LastFM

A gateway to the popular music community. Allows you to send the music you are listening and share it with your friends.

Muine

Are you missing a library mode? No problem, the "muine"(*) plugin provides a (reasonably good) library interface for Eina.

It stores the songs you listen and groups them by artist or by album, allowing you to search play or queue them.

* The name of this plugin is a tribute to an ancient music player called "Muine"

Fieshta

In every party there is a time when people start to skip others and no song is completely played.

This plugin enforces some restrictions while playing a playlist like "don't allow to seek", "don't jump randomly within the playlist", and so.

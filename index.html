    • ★ Your music belongs to you

    Current music players are focused on managing your music collection, while using mandatory criteria about how they organize it.

    Eina does not care about it at all. If your are an organized person, chances are your music collection is organized too, and you won't like to spend your time rearranging it to follow your player's specific rules. In addition, having those players to arrange music that's randomly stored, named or tagged, is usually counterproductive.

    ★ Get all functionality

    Eina works like a common portable music player. Just plays what you want to listen.

    Apart from simplicity, today users expect more from a player than just reading files. Artwork, lyrics, network support, artist data and other music information find their right place in Eina.

    Plugins can take care of all the extra functionality, since Eina exposes everything from its internals. It's fairly easy to extend the player capabilities and change the way it works inside. Feel free to request new features and make things the way you like.

    ★ Based on free technology

    Eina is written in plain C, using Gtk+-3.0 for the graphic interface and GStreamer for playback.

    Gtk means portatility, accesibility, and internationalization. GStreamer means extensibility and support for every audio format out there. These two elements make Eina an unlimited application.

    By the way, Eina is also free (as in free beer) software. Eina is licensed under GPL-2 or later.

    Minimal screen footprint

    In its default state, Eina tries to reduce the space used for displaying information about your music. Music is meant to be listened, not viewed. Reclaim your desktop space.

    Extremely efficient

    Eina has been highly optimized to do what matters: playing music. We are proud to keep the memory and CPU usage at a minimum so you can enjoy Eina in every computer.

    There's no need for a library

    Our philosophy is all about playing music. Eina will not try to organize your music collection in its own way; it won't even try to build a library out of your files. Just drag the files or folders containing what you want to play and let it do its job. Eina does provide history and search functions for your convenience.

    Get the most out of your music

    We also love details. Even though Eina's main goal is to play your music, there's still room for some eyecandy. Eina is bundled with plugins to show your music artwork and lyrics.

    Linux and OS X compatible

    Choose your favorite operative system. Eina is offered for several Linux distributions as well as for Mac OS X.

    Desktop integration

    System tray minimization, desktop notifications, multimedia keys…

    Plugins

    Artwork and lyrics are just two of the plugins bundled with the player. Discover a whole world of features provided through the powerful plugin api. Muine playlist, last.fm integration, playing history, party mode… you name it!

    Themes

    Choose between a bright and dark theme to better integrate Eina into your desktop.
